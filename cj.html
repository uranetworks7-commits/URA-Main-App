Game Rules

  • If you press the Back button during the game, you will be unable to join, and your opponent will win.
  • If the opponent player does not take damage, they may be offline or have exited the game.
  • Your internet connection must be fast for better gameplay.
  • You can also play with your friends or public chat users. Just confirm your room code; both players enter the same room code and their respective names, then join the same game and play 1 vs 1. If any player quits, the room is wasted.
  • Hacking is not allowed And The Player Win The Match Who Kill 2 First.
